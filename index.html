About

Mark Levitt

I'm a product manager with twenty years of experience in platform products, APIs, and developer programs at companies including Betfair, Visa Europe, FanDuel, and OpenText.

My background spans the technical and commercial sides of the role. I've taken APIs from zero to revenue, moved teams from 6-monthly release cycles to continuous delivery, and evaluated platform options at the point where the wrong decision would cost millions. Along the way I've been part of some high-profile outcomes — Visa Europe's €16.5 billion acquisition, FanDuel's $158 million sale to Paddy Power Betfair, and Betfair's £1.5 billion IPO.

Through Deburson Limited, I work on engagements with a specific, measurable goal: a platform defined and ready to build, a team shipping reliably toward clear business objectives, or a technology decision you can commit to with confidence. Every engagement starts by agreeing what good looks like.

Services

What you get

I work with businesses that need senior product experience without a permanent headcount commitment. Each engagement is scoped around a specific outcome.

Platform & API Product Strategy

A clear answer on what your platform needs to do, a pricing model that works commercially, and a roadmap your engineering team can actually execute. I've worked this problem at Betfair and OpenText — from initial pricing models through billing infrastructure, developer beta programs, and launch.

Technology Evaluation & Discovery

A defensible recommendation before you commit significant budget — with commercial, technical, and operational analysis to back it up. Useful when you need independent thinking, not a consultant who'll validate whatever you've already decided.

Product Team Effectiveness

A team that ships regularly toward clear business objectives. I've moved teams from 6-monthly release cycles to continuous delivery at Visa Europe, and realigned platform roadmaps at FanDuel to tie directly to retention and revenue goals.

Interim & Contract Product Management

Product management coverage that's productive quickly — no lengthy onboarding, no ramp-up period. Useful when a hiring gap or a specific initiative needs someone who can establish the right success metrics and start moving toward them.

Experience

FanDuel
2015 — 2019

Product Director, Platform Products

Led platform product for a business with 7 million registered users across 43 US states. The notifications platform I shipped improved customer retention by 12% in its first six months. Visa Europe

Betfair
2005 — 2013

Technical Product Manager & Developer Advocate

Simplified the pricing structure to remove barriers to adoption, delivered a closed beta to validate the new product before full release, and built a developer program that gave the API a commercial identity beyond its technical functionality.
